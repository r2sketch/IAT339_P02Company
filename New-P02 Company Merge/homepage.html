A LITTLE ABOUT JANE

owner and principal planner

I started my wedding planning business, Love & Confetti, in 2014 after realizing the importance of a Day Of Coordinator at my own wedding. Since then, I have moved my business from the lower mainland to beautiful Squamish BC and I haven’t looked back.

Whether your dream is to get married on top of a mountain with breathtaking views, on a riverside resort with the sound of the Cheakamus River in the background or in the middle a lush green ecological reserve, Squamish is the place to have your wedding!

My favourite part of being a wedding planner? Being a part of the happiest day in a couple’s life. There’s nothing like celebrating a couple’s love surrounded by their family and friends.
