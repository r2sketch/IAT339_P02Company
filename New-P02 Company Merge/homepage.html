WATCH YOUR VISION COME TO LIFE

Wouldn’t it be great to be a guest at your own wedding and watch your vision come to life? As an experienced wedding coordination and planning company, Love & Confetti is that someone you can trust to pull off what you’ve always hoped for.

Whether you’re looking for someone to help you from A-Z or you just need someone to help you with the last stretch of planning, we’re here for you!

If you’re not from the area, and want to have your destination wedding in Squamish or Whistler, you’ve come to the right place.
